Iteration 11 change log
========================

Chosen item: potential_improvement_6

Change: Set APPLY_DAYS_SANITIZE from False to True so the 365243 sentinel
in DAYS_EMPLOYED (and other DAYS_ columns) is replaced with NaN inside
encode_features() before LightGBM sees the application-table rows.

Why this is expected to lift validation ROC-AUC: probe_result_10 plateaus
in the 0.7805-0.7806 band from ~epoch 145 onward; the model is well-tuned
but still ingests literal 365243 values (≈1000 years) for the ~55k
applicants with no employment history. That huge outlier dominates split
gains for DAYS_EMPLOYED and warps interactions with related demographic
features (DAYS_BIRTH, DAYS_REGISTRATION, etc.). Mapping the sentinel to
NaN lets LightGBM's native missing-value handling place those rows on a
learned split direction instead of treating them as a numeric extreme.
This is the classic Home Credit fix and routinely adds a few thousandths
of AUC. Treated as a boolean toggle under the always-fully-applied rule.

Step 0 revert: Not performed. probe_result_10 tail_mean (0.7805) is
higher than probe_result_9 tail_mean (0.7770) for higher_is_better, so
iteration 10 (TEST_SIZE 0.5 -> 0.2) was a clear improvement, not a
regression.
