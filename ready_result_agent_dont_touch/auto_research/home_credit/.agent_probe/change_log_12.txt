Change: Lowered LEARNING_RATE from 0.2 to 0.1, halving the per-round step
size of LightGBM's gradient boosting while leaving N_ESTIMATORS=250 and
EARLY_STOPPING_ROUNDS=10 untouched.

Why this is expected to lift validation ROC-AUC: probe_result_10 plateaus
in the 0.7805 band — the model is well-tuned but learning at lr=0.2 takes
coarse steps, so the late-epoch curve oscillates instead of refining
toward a higher peak. Cutting the learning rate in half lets each tree
make finer corrections, which the comment notes is the standard high-AUC
recipe for Home Credit (routinely +0.005-0.015 AUC over lr=0.2). A 2x
reduction is the moderate Regime B step (the curve is well above baseline
and climbing) and is small enough that the existing N_ESTIMATORS=250 cap
should still be sufficient for the model to converge before training is
truncated, while staying within the 2x-5x guidance and moving roughly
half the distance toward the comment's 0.02-0.05 target band.
